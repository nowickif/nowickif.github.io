About Me

Throughout my life, I considered 3 places my local residence: Poland, London, and Pittsburgh. Academic and personal motivations are what made roam between these places.

Having met some incredible people, gathered a variety experiences, and immersed myself in these local environments, I was shaped by all of these places to a significant extent.

I am excited to continue discovering the world and the people around me as I try navigate my future. And the future is exciting!

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University and will begin my second Master's program in Engineering Management at Dartmouth College in Fall 2022.

In future, I hope to combine my engineering background with business and entrepreneurship studies, to lead exciting technology-oriented initiatives!
