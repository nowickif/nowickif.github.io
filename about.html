About Me

Throughout my life, I considered 3 places my local residence: Poland, London, and Pittsburgh. Academic and personal motivations are what made me roam between these places, and I was significantly shaped by them all. I have met incredible people, gathered empowering experiences, and immersed myself in these environments.

These people include friends, professors, and role models, all of whom begin as strangers, but end up making a significant impact on me. The various experiences, on the other hand, include academics within diverse fields of study, exporing the cultural realm of the place I find myself in, and getting involved in impactful projects, such as university mentoring, student research groups, and politics.

I will cherish every moment of my life's journey so far, and I am also so excited to continue discovering the world and the people around me as I try to navigate my future. And let me tell you, the future is exciting!

In the Fall of 2022, I will take all my experiences with me to the East Coast, US, where I will begin my second Master's program, in Engineering Management at Dartmouth College! I could not be more thrilled about this prospect, and that is particularly as I gained considerable high-tech background through my Master's studies revolving around AI at Carnegie Mellon University, and my robotics internship at United Robots.

All of the above intends to brings me closer to realizing the vision and dreams about who I am and who I hope to become. In the personal context, working toward making others happy, finding happiness and fulfillment in the people around me, and all the while continuously developing and improving as a person. In the professional context, I hope to combine my engineering background with business and product management studies to lead exciting technology-oriented initiatives, wherever in the world that is going to be!
